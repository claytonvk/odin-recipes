Plant-Based Fettuccine Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb (450g) fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup (240ml) raw cashews
  • 1 1/2 cups (360ml) water
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) nutritional yeast
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp (30ml) olive oil
  • 1 tbsp (15ml) lemon juice
  • Fresh parsley or basil, chopped (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Start by soaking the cashews in hot water for at least 30 minutes. If you have a high-speed blender, you can skip this step.
  2. Cook the fettuccine according to package instructions.
  3. In a blender, combine the soaked cashews, water, nutritional yeast, garlic, salt, black pepper, olive oil, and lemon juice. Blend until the mixture is smooth and creamy.
  4. In a saucepan over low heat, pour the blended mixture and heat until the sauce thickens, stirring occasionally for about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Once the sauce has thickened, pour it over the cooked fettuccine, and mix well.
  6. Serve the pasta hot, garnished with chopped parsley or basil, if desired.

Enjoy your delicious Whole Food Plant-Based Fettuccine Alfredo!
